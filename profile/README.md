## Key Features

This Luminar Neo Review highlights the essential AI photo editing tools that define the user experience.

* **AI Sky Replacement** — This core feature of Luminar Neo intelligently detects the horizon and replaces dull skies with dramatic sunsets or clear blue vistas. The AI photo engine automatically adjusts the foreground lighting and colors to match the new sky, creating a seamless composite.
* **Relight AI** — A standout function in any Luminar Neo Review, this tool acts as a 3D depth map for light. It allows users to independently adjust the brightness of the foreground subject and the background environment, fixing flat lighting in AI photo compositions with precision.
* **Portrait Bokeh AI** — Luminar Neo excels at portrait editing by using AI photo recognition to identify faces and bodies. It applies a realistic, creamy background blur without the need for complex manual masking or expensive fast glass.
* **Remove Powerlines & Dust Spots** — This AI photo utility automatically scans an image for distracting wires and sensor spots, erasing them with a single click. It saves an immense amount of time compared to manual cloning or healing brushes.
* **Layers and Masking** — While Luminar Neo is known for AI photo automation, it offers a flexible layer-based architecture. This allows for advanced blending modes and the ability to stack multiple AI photo adjustments for complex creative looks.

## Configuration

Configuring the environment for this Luminar Neo Review and testing requires specific system awareness. While Luminar Neo is a standalone application, users should ensure their hardware meets the demands of intensive AI photo processing.

Key configuration notes for running Luminar Neo effectively include:
* **Graphics Acceleration:** Ensure GPU drivers are fully updated. Luminar Neo relies heavily on the GPU for rendering AI photo previews and applying real-time effects.
* **Catalog Location:** Users can configure the location of the Luminar Neo Catalog folder to ensure fast read/write speeds, preferably on a fast internal SSD rather than an external or network drive.
* **Plugin Support:** Luminar Neo can be configured as a plugin for Adobe Lightroom Classic and Photoshop, allowing for a round-trip AI photo workflow.
* **Performance Settings:** Within the application, adjusting the preview quality and proxy rendering settings can significantly improve the responsiveness of the AI photo editor on older machines.
